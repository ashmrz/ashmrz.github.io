Research

As a Research Scientist at Snap Inc., I am deeply passionate about content creation and advancing the frontiers of 3D/4D generative models. My work focuses on developing cutting-edge solutions for reconstructing, generating, and editing dynamic 3D scenes, empowering intuitive and creative workflows. By leveraging large-scale generative models, I strive to transform how we create and interact with 3D and 4D content, blending technology with creativity to unlock new possibilities. Prior to joining Snap, I completed my PhD at the University of Toronto, where I was fortunate to be advised by Igor Gilitschenski and Jonathan Kelly.
